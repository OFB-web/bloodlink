About BloodLink

A Platform Built
to Save Lives

Hospitals and patients in The Gambia often struggle to quickly locate blood donors during emergencies. Phone calls, personal networks, and family outreach are slow — and in emergencies, every minute counts.

BloodLink creates a centralized digital registry where voluntary donors can register and be found instantly by blood group and location.

The platform is fully accessible on mobile and desktop — reaching donors and patients wherever they are.

Built with modern web technologies, the system is fast, simple, and designed for people with varying levels of digital literacy.

How It Works

Three Simple Steps

BloodLink makes the process of finding or registering as a blood donor straightforward and fast.

1

Register as a Donor

Create a free donor profile in minutes. Provide your name, blood group, location, and contact details. Your availability helps others find you when it matters most.

2

Search by Blood Group

Filter the donor directory by blood type to instantly find compatible donors near you. Results show availability, location, and contact information.

3

Connect & Save Lives

Contact the donor directly through their provided details. Every connection made through BloodLink is a potential life saved in a critical moment.

Platform Features

Everything You Need,
Nothing You Don't

BloodLink is designed to be powerful yet simple — focused entirely on the one thing that matters: connecting donors with patients fast.

Blood Group Search

Instantly filter the donor registry by blood group — A+, A-, B+, B-, O+, O-, AB+, AB-. Find compatible donors with a single click during any emergency.

Simple Donor Registration

A clean, easy-to-fill form lets anyone register as a blood donor in under two minutes. No technical expertise required — accessible to everyone.

Direct Donor Contact

Donor profiles display contact information so patients or hospital staff can reach out directly — no middleman, no delays. Speed is everything.

Community Donor Network

Every registered donor becomes part of a growing community of life-savers across The Gambia — building a resilient healthcare support network together.

Mobile-First Responsive Design

Fully optimised for smartphones, tablets, and desktops — so anyone can find a donor or register from any device, anywhere in The Gambia.

Availability Status Updates

Donors can update their availability status in real time, ensuring patients only see donors who are currently able to help — reducing wasted effort in emergencies.
